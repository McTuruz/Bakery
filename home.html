What we offer

Joseph's Bakery offers a variaty of pastry products. In our stone shops, we make fresh products every morning and all that is left at the end of the day we donate to homeless shelters.

Also, we make custom products on order. From giant cakes to cute biscuites. It all depends on YOUR imagination!!

Interesting facts!

Ancient Egyptians were the first ones to come up with the idea of pastries. Called baklava and filo, they were made out of grain meal with honey, fruits and spices.
